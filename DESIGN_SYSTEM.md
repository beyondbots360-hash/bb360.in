# Beyond Bots Design System

Version: 1.0
Domain: [www.bb360.in](http://www.bb360.in)

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Beyond Bots is not a generic marketing agency.

The visual identity should communicate:
* Intelligence
* Engineering Excellence
* Business Automation
* Reliability
* Scalability
* Enterprise Readiness

The design language should feel closer to a modern technology company than a traditional agency.

Examples of inspiration:
* Stripe
* Linear
* Vercel
* Notion
* OpenAI

Avoid:
* Overused AI neon effects
* Excessive gradients
* Cartoon illustrations
* Stock-photo-heavy layouts
* Generic marketing templates

Preferred:
* Bold typography
* Clean spacing
* Geometric layouts
* Strong contrast
* Minimal but purposeful animation

---

# COLOR USAGE RULES

Primary Ratio:
* 70% White
* 20% Black
* 10% Brand Orange

Orange should be used strategically. Do not flood the page with orange.
Orange should draw attention to:
* Buttons
* Links
* Interactive elements
* Important statistics

# ANIMATION RULES

# FINAL VISUAL IMPRESSION

When users visit Beyond Bots, they should immediately feel:
"These people build serious technology solutions that can help my business scale."

Not:
"Another marketing agency."

The design should communicate trust, technical expertise, automation, and business growth through a clean modern interface centered around the Beyond Bots identity and the BB360 ecosystem.
