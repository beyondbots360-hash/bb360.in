# Beyond Bots – Site Architecture & SEO Blueprint

This document defines the structural blueprint, content blocks, internal links, semantic heading hierarchy, and targeted keyword mappings for **Beyond Bots** (`www.bb360.in`).

---

## 1. Global SEO & Metadata Specifications

- **Domain:** `https://www.bb360.in`
- **Primary Target Audience:** Startups, Small Businesses, and Mid-Market Enterprises in India.
- **Secondary Target Audience:** Operations managers and business decision-makers in the USA & UK seeking cost-effective automation and digital transformation.
- **Global Metadata Config:**
  - **Meta Title:** `Beyond Bots | AI Automation, Web Development & Digital Growth Solutions`
  - **Meta Description:** `Beyond Bots helps businesses scale with custom web development, AI-powered automation, agentic solutions, and digital marketing services. Build smarter, grow faster.`
  - **Meta Keywords:** `AI Automation Services India, Agentic AI Solutions, Web Development Company India, Digital Marketing Agency India, AI Powered Business Solutions, Website Development Services, Business Process Automation`.
  - **Robots Config:** Allow index/follow, referencing sitemap.xml.
  - **Canonical URL:** `https://www.bb360.in`
